Scenarios for Persona 1(Cassandra)

Scenario 1:
Cooking more home cooked meals
Objective: Cassandra wants to make good quality meals that remind her of her childhood southern roots.
Persona Reference: Cassandra is determined to prioritize her well-being and likes to make homecooked meals that remind her of her little Mississippi hometown.
Activity: Cassanda uses FlavorFind to find southern meals like her family used to make.
Issues: The existing system lacks features that allow the user to search for specific dishes under a meal catagory.
Solution: FlavorFind can create a search that will link meals that the user searches for with similar ones.

Scenario 2:
Cooking more meals that are quick and simple
Objective: Cassandra wants to make her life easier with simple meals that she can make in between work and studying.
Persona Reference: During the week Cassandra would like more simple meals that can be made in one pot or even her new obsession the crockpot.
Activity:Cassandra searches FlavorFind for meals that only require one pot or even use a crockpot.
Issues: The existing system lacks the ability to reduce the results to only meals that use a specific cooking tool.
Solution: FlavorFind could impliment a way to create a linked search that allows users to sort by tools required to cook the meals.

Scenario 3:
Low carb meal prep for the week
Objective: Cassandra needs help in preparing low carb meals for her long study nights after working all day and ensuring she has nutritious options readily available.
Persona Reference: Cassandra has even thought about meal prepping on the weekends to help reduce her stress throughtout the week when it comes to cooking meals.
Activity: Cassandra uses FlavorFind to search for good recipes for meal prepping. She is looking for dishes that can be easily cooked, stored, and reheated during her busy days.
Issues: The existing system lacks a dedicated section for meal prep recipes, making it hard for Cassandra to find good options for her needs.
Solution: FlavorFind could introduce a "Meal Prep" category and even sort by meals that are low-carb, curating recipes that are suitable for preparing in advance and storing.

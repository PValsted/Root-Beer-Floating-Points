Scenarios for Persona 1(Emily)

Scenario 1:
Quick and Nutritious Meal Planning Objective: Emily wants to plan quick and nutritious meals for her upcoming work week to ensure she maintains energy levels during her demanding nursing shifts. Persona Reference: Emily Turner, a dedicated 23-year-old registered nurse with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing, values high-protein and nutritious meals to support her busy schedule. Activity: Emily logs into FlavorFind to explore meal options that are not only quick to prepare but also meet her nutritional requirements. She uses the search filters to find recipes with high-protein content and ingredients that align with her dietary preferences. Issues: Emily encounters a challenge in finding recipes that strike the right balance between quick preparation and nutritional value. The existing system lacks a feature to customize recipes based on specific dietary needs and time constraints. Solution: FlavorFind could introduce a personalized meal planning feature, allowing users like Emily to input their dietary preferences, time constraints, and ingredient availability. The system then generates a customized weekly meal plan with recipes tailored to the user's requirements.

Scenario 2:
Exploring New Cooking Techniques Objective: Emily is interested in expanding her cooking skills and trying out new cooking techniques during her leisure time. Persona Reference: Emily Turner, a registered nurse, is open to exploring new hobbies and skills outside of work, including her passion for cooking. Activity: Emily browses FlavorFind for recipes that involve unique cooking techniques or ingredients she hasn't used before. She seeks variety and challenges in her cooking to make the most of her downtime. Issues: The existing system lacks a feature to highlight recipes that focus on specific cooking techniques or introduce users to new culinary methods. Solution: FlavorFind could introduce a "Cooking Techniques" category, featuring recipes that emphasize specific cooking methods. This allows users like Emily to explore new skills and techniques, enhancing their cooking expertise.

Scenario 3:
Meal Prep for Long Shifts Objective: Emily needs assistance in planning and preparing meals for her long nursing shifts, ensuring she has nutritious options readily available. Persona Reference: Emily Turner, a registered nurse with demanding shifts, values high-protein and nutritious meals to sustain her energy throughout the day. Activity: Emily uses FlavorFind to search for recipes suitable for meal prepping. She looks for dishes that can be easily portioned, stored, and reheated during her busy workdays. Issues: The existing system lacks a dedicated section for meal prep-friendly recipes, making it challenging for Emily to find suitable options for her specific needs. Solution: FlavorFind could introduce a "Meal Prep" category, curating recipes that are suitable for preparing in advance and storing. This feature would help users like Emily efficiently plan and execute their meal prep routines for busy workweeks.

Elevate your meals with simplicity and flavor

Interview date:

January 14, 2024

Interviewer:

Chris Stirneman

What types of cuisines or dishes do you enjoy cooking the most?

"I enjoy cooking a variety of cuisines, but I have a soft spot for Italian and American dishes."

Are there any specific dietary restrictions or preferences you have, such as vegetarian, gluten-free, or low-carb?

"I want to learn more healthy, high protein, and low-calorie recipes."

How much time do you usually have for meal preparation on a regular basis?

"I have limited time where I could meal prep or spend time in the kitchen due to my busy schedule."

Do you prefer quick and easy recipes or are you willing to invest more time for elaborate dishes?

"I have always cooked quick, simple meals. However, I am very interested in learning more complex dishes and would be okay with spending a little longer in the kitchen."

Are there any ingredients you particularly love or dislike?

"I love experimenting with fresh herbs and spices, and I usually choose chicken as my protein. I don't usually like meals that require a lot of oil."

How skilled would you say you are in the kitchen, ranging from beginner to advanced?

"I would say I am more of an intermediate level cook. I can make simple meals and love dishes that don't require too much effort."

Do you have any kitchen appliances or tools that you frequently use and would like recipes for?

"I would love to learn more dishes for the air fryer or crockpot."

Are there specific occasions or events for which you often cook, and what type of recipes are you looking for on those occasions?

"I love making deserts for the holidays and side dishes/snacks for other events."

Do you prefer recipes with common and easily accessible ingredients, or are you open to trying unique or exotic ingredients?

"I typically use the basic spices and ingredients, but I am open to exploring more ingredients."

How often do you like to try new recipes, and what motivates you to explore new dishes?

"I like to try new recipes, but I usually don't. What motivates me is seeing new dishes on social media."

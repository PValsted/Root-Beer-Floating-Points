User Stories (Cassandra)

"As a busy individual managing work and studies, I want FlavorFind to provide quick and easy recipes that can be prepared within 30 to 45 minutes, enabling me to balance my schedule efficiently."

"As someone who likes different types of food, I would like FlavorFind to offer a variety of dishes, enabling me to explore and master these cuisines in my pursuit of culinary creativity"

"As a person eating a low carb diet, I seek FlavorFind to recommend low-carb options in its recipes, aligning with my dietary preferences and helping me maintain a healthy lifestyle amid my demanding work and study routine."

"As a user who like to be efficent, I desire FlavorFind to suggest one-pot meal recipes specifically designed for crockpots, facilitating convenient preparation and allowing me to enjoy flavorful dishes without compromising on my time."

"As someone with limited time but a desire to enhance cooking skills, I want FlavorFind to provide mid-level cooking challenges that are manageable within my time constraints."

"As a novice cook, I look to FlavorFind to provide mid-level cooking challenges, helping me expand my culinary skills gradually while ensuring the recipes remain manageable within my time constraints."

"As someone who likes to plan means ahead, I am interested in FlavorFind's feature to highlight easily accessible ingredients, allowing me to confidently choose recipes with familiar items and streamline my cooking process."

"As someone who wants to try new foods, I hope FlavorFind inspires me with new and interesting recipes every 1-2 months, offering a variety that aligns with my occasional exploration of culinary delights beyond my regular cooking routine."

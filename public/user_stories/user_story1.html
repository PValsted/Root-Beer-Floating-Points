User Stories (Emily)

"As a registered nurse, I want to discover quick and nutritious recipes on FlavorFind to support my busy schedule and maintain energy levels during demanding shifts."

"As a cooking enthusiast, I want FlavorFind to provide a variety of recipes with unique cooking techniques, allowing me to explore new culinary skills during my leisure time."

"As a health-conscious individual, I want FlavorFind to offer personalized meal planning options, considering my dietary preferences and time constraints, to simplify the process of preparing nutritious meals."

"As a user with a busy lifestyle, I want FlavorFind to introduce a "Meal Prep" category, featuring recipes suitable for preparing in advance and storing, to assist me in planning meals for long nursing shifts."

"As a FlavorFind user, I want the system to notify me of new recipes or cooking tips that align with my preferences, ensuring that I stay updated on the latest culinary inspiration."

"As a registered nurse, I want to be able to save and organize my favorite recipes on FlavorFind, creating a personalized recipe collection that I can easily access for future meal planning."

"As a user with specific dietary needs, I want FlavorFind to include filters for allergen-free or specialized diets, ensuring that I can find recipes tailored to my health requirements."

"As a FlavorFind user, I want the ability to share my favorite recipes with a community of healthcare professionals, creating a space for recipe exchange and collaboration among nurses and other healthcare workers."

Elevate your meals with simplicity and flavor

Interview date:

January 15, 2024

Interviewer:

Patrick Valsted

What types of cuisines or dishes do you enjoy cooking the most?

"I mostly love to cook American or Mexican food the most."


Are there any specific dietary restrictions or preferences you have, such as vegetarian, gluten-free, or low-carb?

"I nor anyone I know have any dietary restrictions, so I cook with no restrictions."


How much time do you usually have for meal preparation on a regular basis?

"On days I work, I tend to devote 2 hours to meal prep. When it comes to the weekends, I might spend all day on it if it's something I'm passionate about making. My chihuahua Moonpie is my little helper."


Do you prefer quick and easy recipes or are you willing to invest more time for elaborate dishes?

"It depends on if I want to make something from scratch. If so, I'm willing to invest more time if it's worth it to do so. Otherwise, it's better to work smarter not harder sometimes."


Are there any ingredients you particularly love or dislike?

"I LOVE to use butter and cheese. You can never have too much cheese. I also love to use heavy whipping cream. I'm not picky about food so there are no ingredients I dislike."


How skilled would you say you are in the kitchen, ranging from beginner to advanced?

"I would say I'm intermediate-advanced."


Do you have any kitchen appliances or tools that you frequently use and would like recipes for?

"I try to keep things simple and not use any appliances. For example, even though I own a Crock Pot I don't use it often. A wooden spoon and knife is often all I need!"


Are there specific occasions or events for which you often cook, and what type of recipes are you looking for on those occasions?

"I enjoy cooking for any major holiday. I like making Thanksgiving food, and for Christmas I love to bake treats. On Fridays I make meals for my husband's work, so adjusting proportions based on group size is important to me."


Do you prefer recipes with common and easily accessible ingredients, or are you open to trying unique or exotic ingredients?

"Again, I like to keep things simple, so I rarely use ingredients I am unfamiliar with, so I usually just grab things from Walmart. However, on occasion I do shop at a Mexican grocery store."


How often do you like to try new recipes, and what motivates you to explore new dishes?

"I do like to try new recipes weekly, so long as they aren't too strange. What motivates me is cooking for others and seeing them enjoy my dish."

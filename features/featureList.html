Feature List

    Search by Cusine Type

  • Users can explore a diverse range of cuisine types, including but not limited to Italian, Mexican, Asian, and more.

    • Filter by Dietary Restrictions

  • FlavorFind accommodates various dietary restrictions such as vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other allergen considerations.

    • Discover Recipes by Skill Level

  • Users can choose recipes based on skill levels, ranging from beginner-friendly to advanced cooking techniques.

    • Generate a Shopping List

  • FlavorFind generates a convenient shopping list based on selected recipes, simplifying the ingredient acquisition process.
